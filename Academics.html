What academic offerings does Stars British School provide?

Stars British School provides a diverse range of academic offerings to support the intellectual growth and development of its students. These offerings include a comprehensive British Curriculum, highly qualified teachers, enrichment programs, and support services. The school also offers a variety of learning resources, such as a library, computer labs, and online resources, to enhance the academic experience of its students. Additionally, Stars British School provides internationally recognized qualifications, such as Cambridge International Examinations (CIE) and an International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE)

How to track your child's academic level and improvement?

Stars British School allows parents to track their child's academic level and improvement or even send and receive feedback from and to teachers.

All this is done by an application called scl which greets parents with a dashboard filled out with academic data about grades and improvement in comparison to previous terms. parents also have the ability to access Homeworks sent to students through the application ask teachers about anything about their child's academics or behaviour.
